About JungFrau Group

JUNGFRAU is an integrated group of companies, We Carry out services in the following areas, but not limited to International Trade (import/export ) , Manufacturers Raw Materials & Agro commodities Export, Vessel Management, Freight forwarding, marine logistics and support services.

Our Vision

To generate massive food production to feed the world and make people have sustainably economies’ and become happy. To make the world a market place for Nigerian non-oil products. To sustain Africa’s leading position as the world food basket. To ensure that we are one-source destination for the manufacturer’s raw materials. To sustain a well preserved integrity in efficient and effective customer services relations, ensuring that products and services are of international standards and best practices.

Mission and Strategy

To supply quality products to our business partners all over the globe, in an effective manner. As a posterity for legacy" To spearhead the diversification of the Nigerian economy by expanding and increasing non-oil exports for sustainable and inclusive economic growth. To foster market -oriented, private sector-driven economy through market oriented export products development. To sustain the leadership position we have for agro export and international trade. To continue investing on research methodologies, involving brands strategies and management
